Welcome To Green Heart Organics

At Green Heart Organics, we're passionate about sustainable solutions, and vermicomposting with Eisenia fetida worms, also known as red wigglers, is a favorite! The windrow method is ideal for farms, gardens, and anyone wanting to efficiently transform organic waste into nutrient-rich vermicompost, all on a larger scale.

Ready to get your hands dirty?

Let's get started!

Gather your tools:

01

Open Area: Find a well-ventilated, protected spot outdoors, like a shaded garden corner or covered concrete pad. Bedding Materials:Get coarse materials like shredded leaves, cardboard, and straw - your wiggly friends adore a cozy home! Aim for a pile 4-6 feet wide and 3-4 feet tall. Organic Waste: Diversify your worms' menu with food scraps, yard waste, and even manure (avoid meat, dairy, and oily foods). Red Wigglers: Purchase these happy composters from trusted worm suppliers. Start with about 1 pound per square meter of windrow space.

Building your Windrow:

02

Lay the foundation:Block out unwanted guests with weed barrier mesh to protect your precious ecosystem. Bedding bonanza: Pile up your chosen bedding materials, gently moistening them as you go. Wiggle welcome:Scatter your red wigglers evenly over the damp bedding. Food fiesta:Start light with food scraps and yard waste, burying them under the bedding for a delightful surprise. Monitoring your masterpiece:Keep things pleasantly moist, not soggy. Every 1-2 weeks, give the pile a good turn, ensuring air circulation and even distribution of worms and food.

Harvesting your Green Gold:

03

After 3-6 months, your windrow will be transformed! Dark, crumbly, and earthy-smelling compost awaits. Harvest from the edges, leaving the active composting zone undisturbed. Scoop up your bounty and apply it directly to your garden or potting soil - happy plants guaranteed!

Why choose the Windrow Method?

04

Waste warriors:Large windrows handle big volumes of organic waste, making them perfect for large-scale operations. Low-maintenance heroes: Once set up, windrows require minimal upkeep, just regular turning and moisture checks. Budget-friendly champions: Compared to other methods, windrows are cost-effective and easy to maintain. Nutrient-rich champions:The finished vermicompost is packed with nutrients and beneficial microbes, giving your plants the ultimate boost.

Green Heart Organics' Top Tips:

05

Shade seekers:During hot weather or heavy rain, cover your windrow with shade cloth or burlap to keep temperatures and moisture stable. Worm watch:See fewer worms or slow decomposition? Adjust your feeding habits or moisture levels. Layered approach:Add new food scraps in thin layers, preventing overwhelm for your hardworking crew. Patience is key:Don't rush! Vermicomposting takes time. Let your windrow work its magic and reap the rewards.

Our services

Vermicompost

Vermicompost is the product of the decomposition process using various species of worms, usually red wigglers.

Vermiwash

Vermiwash is a liquid extract produced from vermicompost in a medium where earthworms are richly populated.

Earth worms or Red Wigglers

Purchase these happy composters from us. Start with about 1 pound per square meter of windrow space.
