Who We Are?

About the Owner

Hey there, I'm Shiv Kumar, the founder and managing director of Green Heart Organics. As a graduate with a B.Voc in Retail Management from H.P.U., I started my career as a fashion consultant at Nautica India. However, witnessing the growing cancer rates in my state due to excessive fertilizer use, I felt compelled to make a difference. Driven by the desire to save lives and revolutionize Indian agriculture, I founded Green Heart Organics in 2023. Today, we are a leading provider of high-quality vermicompost, consulting services, and training programs for farmers transitioning to organic methods. Our mission is to empower farmers and consumers through sustainable practices, contributing to a healthier future for all. By 2025, we aim to increase organic farming acreage in India by 20%, providing healthier produce and supporting the livelihoods of 5,000 farmers. Join us on this journey! Learn more about our products and services, read inspiring stories from our farmers, and discover how you can contribute to a more sustainable future at green heart organics.

Our services

Vermicompost

Vermicompost is the product of the decomposition process using various species of worms, usually red wigglers.

Vermiwash

Vermiwash is a liquid extract produced from vermicompost in a medium where earthworms are richly populated.

Earth worms or Red Wigglers

Purchase these happy composters from us. Start with about 1 pound per square meter of windrow space.
